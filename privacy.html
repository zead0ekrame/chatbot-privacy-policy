2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our chatbot:

  • Messages and Inputs: The questions, requests, commands, or any text you send to the bot.
  • Facebook Profile Information: Your name, profile picture, and Facebook ID as provided by the Messenger Platform API.
  • Conversation Data: Chat history, message timestamps, and interaction patterns.

Note: We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our chatbot service. We do not access your Facebook friends list, posts, or any other personal information beyond what is explicitly shared in our conversation.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • To improve the chatbot's accuracy and user experience.
  • To analyze anonymized usage patterns for service enhancement.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3.1. Messaging Policy (Meta Compliance)

24-Hour Messaging Window: We strictly comply with Meta's Standard Messaging policies:

  • Automated Messages: We only send automated messages within 24 hours of the user's last interaction.
  • Post-24-Hour Messages: Any messages sent after 24 hours will only use Meta-approved tags such as:

    • Service notifications

  • Opt-in Required: Marketing messages require explicit user consent (opt-in) with easy opt-out options.
