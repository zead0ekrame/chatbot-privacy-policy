Your Right to Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. Under applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

What This Means: You can request that we permanently delete all personal data we have collected about you through our chatbot service, including conversation history, profile information, and usage analytics.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Conversation Data: All chat messages, questions, and interactions with the chatbot
  • Profile Information: Your name, profile picture, and Facebook ID from Messenger
  • Usage Analytics: Interaction patterns, frequency data, and response times
  • Technical Data: Device information, browser data, and IP addresses
  • Account Data: Any stored preferences or settings associated with your account

How to Request Data Deletion

To delete your data, please follow these simple steps:

1 Automated Data Deletion Request
Use our automated deletion endpoint:
POST https://ekramy-ai.online/user-data-deletion
Or send an email to ziad@ekramy-ai.online with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
2 Include Required Information
In your email, please provide:
  • Your full name
  • The platform you used (Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, etc.)
  • Your user ID or profile identifier (if known)
  • Date range for deletion (if you want partial deletion)
3 Verification Process
We may need to verify your identity to protect your privacy. This may include:
  • Confirming your email address
  • Verifying your identity through the platform you used
  • Additional security questions if necessary
4 Confirmation and Processing
Once verified, we will:
  • Send you a confirmation email or API response
  • Provide a tracking URL and confirmation code
  • Begin the deletion process immediately
  • Complete deletion within 48 hours
  • Send you a final confirmation when complete
API Response Format:
{ url: "https://ekramy-ai.online/delete/[confirmation_code]", confirmation_code: "[unique_code]" }

Deletion Timeline

Standard Processing Time: We commit to processing your data deletion request within 48 hours of receiving a verified request.

Our deletion process includes:

  • Immediate (0-1 hour): Removal from active databases and systems
  • Within 24 hours: Deletion from backup systems and caches
  • Within 48 hours: Complete removal from all storage locations and archives
  • Ongoing (weekly): Regular audits to ensure complete deletion
  • Verification (monthly): Confirmation that deleted data remains unrecoverable

What Happens After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • Permanent Removal: Your data cannot be recovered or restored
  • Service Impact: You may need to re-enter basic information if you use the service again
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data may be retained for service improvement
  • Legal Records: We may retain minimal records if required by law
Important: Data deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once completed, we cannot restore your data or conversation history.

Alternative Data Management Options

If you don't want to delete all your data, you can also:

  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Partial Deletion: Request deletion of specific data types only
  • Data Correction: Request updates to inaccurate information
  • Usage Limitation: Request restrictions on how your data is processed

Marketing Preferences (Opt-in/Opt-out)

Marketing Communications: You have full control over marketing messages:

  • Opt-in: Marketing messages require your explicit consent
  • Opt-out: You can unsubscribe at any time via:
    • Reply "STOP" to any marketing message
    • Email us at ziad@ekramy-ai.online
    • Use our chatbot command: "unsubscribe marketing"
  • Immediate Effect: Opt-out requests are processed within 24 hours

Data Retention After Deletion Request

After you submit a deletion request, we will immediately stop processing your data for any purpose other than completing the deletion request. We will only retain the minimum information necessary to:

  • Process your deletion request
  • Verify completion of deletion
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
